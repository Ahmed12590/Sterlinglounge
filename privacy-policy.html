PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Sterling Digital Services collects, uses, protects, and manages information gathered through this website and our services.

By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

When you contact us, place an order, request a quote, or use our services, we may collect personal information such as:

Your name, email address, phone number, billing details, project information, and any materials you provide for your service request.

This information is collected only to communicate with you, process your order, provide services, manage payments, and deliver project-related support.

How We Use Your Information

Sterling Digital Services may use your information to:

  • Process service requests and orders.
  • Communicate project updates and requirements.
  • Provide quotes, invoices, and customer support.
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • Maintain records for business, quality, and service purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Access and Control Over Your Information

You have the right to contact us at any time to:

  • Review the personal information we have about you.
  • Request corrections or updates to your information.
  • Ask us to delete your personal data where applicable.
  • Raise concerns about how your information is being used.
  • Stop receiving future communication from us.

To make any of these requests, you may contact us through the email address, phone number, or contact form provided on our website.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

Access to personal information is limited to employees, team members, or service providers who need it to perform specific business tasks such as billing, customer support, project management, or service delivery.

Computers, files, and systems containing personal information are kept secure. Communications between clients and Sterling Digital Services may be reviewed or recorded for quality, training, and service improvement purposes while maintaining confidentiality.

Payment Information

To process orders, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, billing address, and order details.

Credit card or payment information may be processed securely through third-party payment providers. Sterling Digital Services does not store full credit card details on its own systems unless otherwise required by a secure and authorized payment processor.

Payment-related information is used only to complete transactions, manage invoices, and provide services.

IP Address and Cookies

When you visit our website, certain non-personal information may be automatically collected, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and usage activity.

This information may be used for website performance, analytics, security, server diagnostics, and improving user experience.

Our website may also use cookies to improve browsing experience, remember preferences, and gather general usage statistics. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but some website features may not function properly.

Data Sharing

Sterling Digital Services does not share personal information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Any text messaging opt-in data, consent, or communication preferences will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share limited information only when necessary to provide services, process payments, comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or work with trusted service providers who support our business operations.

Products and Services Information

We make every effort to describe our services accurately on our website.

However, we do not guarantee that all service descriptions, pricing, availability, or website content will always be complete, current, or free from errors.

If you believe any service information is inaccurate or unclear, please contact us before placing an order or making payment.

Confidentiality and Ownership

We respect the privacy and confidentiality of every client project.

All communication, manuscripts, documents, designs, content, ideas, and project materials shared with Sterling Digital Services are treated as confidential and will not be disclosed to unauthorized third parties.

Clients retain ownership rights to the final approved products and services delivered to them, unless otherwise stated in a separate agreement.

Originality of Content

Sterling Digital Services is committed to providing original, professional, and plagiarism-free content.

Any content created for clients is developed with care and is not copied from other sources. If research or reference material is used, it will be properly rewritten, adapted, or cited where necessary to maintain originality and authenticity.

Third-Party Services

Our website or services may include links to third-party websites, tools, payment processors, or platforms.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices, policies, or content of third-party websites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

Sterling Digital Services reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time.

Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of our website or services after updates means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

For questions, concerns, corrections, or data-related requests, please contact Sterling Digital Services through the official contact details provided on our website.
