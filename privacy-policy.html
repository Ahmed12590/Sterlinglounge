Privacy Policy

At STERLING DIGITAL SERVICES, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

To deliver our services efficiently, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business details provided through forms or communication.
  • Business & Project Data: Store credentials, API access, brand assets, content files, and other materials required to perform services.
  • Payment Information: Payments are securely processed through trusted third-party providers. We do not store sensitive payment details such as full credit card numbers on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly for business purposes, including:

  • Delivering and managing the services you request, such as eCommerce solutions, automation, or web development.
  • Communicating updates, project progress, and billing information.
  • Enhancing our services, internal processes, and security measures.

3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We respect your privacy and do not sell or trade your data. Information may only be shared when necessary to complete services, including:

  • Trusted cloud hosting and development tools.
  • Third-party platforms such as eCommerce marketplaces or integrations required for your project.
  • Publishing or technical tools needed for content formatting or distribution.

All third-party partners are carefully selected and required to maintain data confidentiality.

4. Data Security & Confidentiality

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Our team operates under strict confidentiality agreements to ensure that sensitive data — such as business strategies, private label products, and proprietary content — remains fully secure.

5. Your Rights & Control

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Ask for corrections or updates to your information.
  • Request deletion of your data where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:
Email: info@sterlingdigitalservices.agency

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Analyze website traffic and performance.
  • Improve user experience and functionality.

You can manage or disable cookies anytime through your browser settings.

7. Updates to This Policy

STERLING DIGITAL SERVICES may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
